African Bird's Eye is a different chemistry to Asian birds-eye —
smaller pod, longer drying season, and capsaicin levels that
routinely sit above 50,000 SHU. Uganda's northern belt around Lira
and Apac is the country's heartland for ABE; the eastern slopes
contribute a smaller, longer-pod variant prized for paste and
fermentation applications.

We dry exclusively on raised beds with overnight cover. De-stemming
happens by hand on a sorting line at the cooperative's drying centre,
then mechanically again at Namanve for final dust extraction. Whole
pod and crushed-flake formats are produced to spec.
