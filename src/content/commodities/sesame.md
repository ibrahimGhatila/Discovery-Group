Northern Uganda's red-loam belt around Gulu and Lira produces some
of the cleanest, highest-oil sesame in the region. The Discovery
sesame line at Namanve runs pre-cleaning, gravity table separation,
and optical sorting on the same flow — moving from farm-gate at 96%
purity to export at 99.95%+.

We grade to whitish (food-service and bakery), natural (tahini and
oil pressing), and mixed-brown (oil and bird-feed). Organic lots
are processed on a dedicated shift with full traceability and
segregation documentation.
