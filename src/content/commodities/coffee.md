Coffee is Discovery's anchor commodity and the foundation of our origin
relationships. Arabica from the Mt. Elgon and Rwenzori slopes — the
two volcanic systems that define Ugandan specialty — is purchased
parchment from washing stations we audit, then dry-milled, sorted by
screen, density and colour, and lot-built at Namanve before container
stuffing.

For Robusta we control the line from buying centre to FCL. Screen
selection and moisture are verified per bag on our shop floor before
GrainPro sealing.

Every shipment ships with: SCAA-style cup analysis, ICO export
documentation, UCDA Quality Certificate, organic/RA documentation
where applicable, and EUDR geolocation files for European
destinations.
