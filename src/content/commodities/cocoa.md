Uganda's western cocoa belt sits on the same volcanic axis as the
country's specialty coffee — the Rwenzori and Albertine Rift floor.
Fine-flavour Trinitario hybrids grown under shade in Bundibugyo are
the basis of our specialty cocoa programme; Forastero-dominant stock
from Hoima fills our bulk grade.

Fermentation happens in five-box cascades at cooperative-run centres,
with temperature and turning logs maintained per batch. Drying is
exclusively on raised beds — no direct ground or tarpaulin contact.
Final cut-test grading happens at Namanve before bagging.
