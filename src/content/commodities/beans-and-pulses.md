Discovery's pulse line addresses three core demand streams: red
kidney beans for North African and Indian markets, pigeon peas for
South Asia and East Africa, and cowpeas/green grams for Middle East
food-service.

All four are sourced from cooperative-aggregated lots in the
highlands, where elevation and bimodal rainfall give a longer, cooler
ripening window than the lowland equivalents from Tanzania or Kenya.
Cleaning and sorting happens at Namanve through the same line as
sesame, with dedicated bean / pulse changeovers and full kit cleanout
between lots.
